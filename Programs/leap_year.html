Leap Year

This program asks user to enter a year, and returns if it is a Leap Year or not. Any year divisible by 4 is a leap year. However, there is still a small error that must be accounted for, to eliminate this error, year that is divisible by 100 is a leap year only if it is also evenly divisible by 400.
